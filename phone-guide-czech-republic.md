# Phone Setup Guide — Czech Republic
## For: Moving to Ostrava, CZ | iPhone 16

---

## THE SHORT VERSION
Get an Airalo eSIM before you leave. Download WhatsApp. You're done. Total cost: ~$27/month.

---

## STEP 1 — Check Your Phone Is Unlocked
Before anything else, confirm your iPhone is carrier unlocked.

1. Go to **Settings → General → About**
2. Scroll down to **"Carrier Lock"**
3. It should say **"No SIM restrictions"**

If it says locked to AT&T/Verizon/T-Mobile — contact your carrier and ask them to unlock it. They're required to do this if your account is in good standing. Takes 24-48 hours.

---

## STEP 2 — Get an Airalo eSIM (Do This Before You Leave)

**Airalo** is an app that adds an international data plan to your iPhone without needing a physical SIM card.

1. Download the **Airalo app** from the App Store
2. Search for **"Europe"**
3. Buy the **"Eurolink" Europe regional plan** — approximately $27 for 10GB
   - This works in Czech Republic AND across all of Europe
   - Better value than a Czech-only plan
4. Install the eSIM in the app — takes about 2 minutes
5. **Activate it when you land in Prague or Ostrava**

Your US phone number stays active. iMessages and FaceTime still work over WiFi.

---

## STEP 3 — Download WhatsApp
WhatsApp is how everyone communicates in Europe. It's free over data or WiFi.

1. Download **WhatsApp** from the App Store
2. Set it up with your US number before you leave
3. This becomes your primary way to call and text anyone — family, friends, Czech locals

**WhatsApp calls are free** as long as you have data or WiFi. No per-minute charges.

---

## STEP 4 (Optional) — Get a Czech Local SIM for Local Calls
If you need to call Czech businesses (landlord, utilities, Czech phone numbers), you'll want a local number.

- Buy a prepaid SIM at Prague Airport or any **Vodafone, T-Mobile, or O2** store in Ostrava
- Cost: ~500-800 CZK ($22-35) for a month
- They speak English at most carrier stores and will set it up for you

Your iPhone 16 can hold both the Airalo eSIM AND a physical SIM at the same time.

---

## QUICK TIPS

- **Turn off cellular data roaming** on your US carrier before you leave — otherwise you'll get roaming charges accidentally
- To turn off: Settings → Cellular → Cellular Data Options → turn off "Data Roaming"
- Connect to WiFi whenever possible — saves your data allowance
- Video calls (FaceTime, WhatsApp Video) use more data than regular calls
- **Prague airport has free WiFi** — get connected immediately when you land and activate your Airalo eSIM there

---

## IF SOMETHING GOES WRONG

- **Airalo support:** airalo.com/support (live chat)
- **WhatsApp issues:** Call home on iMessage/FaceTime over WiFi
- **Lost phone:** Contact your US carrier immediately to suspend the line
