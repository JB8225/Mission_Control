## THE BUSINESS

**GFS DFW Fire Pros** provides fire suppression, alarm, and life safety system installation, inspection, and monitoring services to commercial clients across the Dallas-Fort Worth Metroplex.

| Metric | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Industry** | Fire Protection & Life Safety Services |
| **Geography** | Dallas-Fort Worth Metroplex, TX |
| **Client Type** | Commercial — property managers, contractors, building owners |
| **Revenue Model** | Recurring inspection contracts + new install + service |
| **Seller** | Woody Jarmon (owner-operated, founder transition) |

**Why Fire Protection?**
- Code-mandated. Every commercial building in Texas must maintain fire protection systems. This is not discretionary spending.
- Recurring revenue. Annual inspections and monitoring contracts create predictable cash flow.
- Fragmented market. Dominated by small owner-operated shops. Consolidation opportunity is significant.
- PE is paying 6–10x EBITDA for these businesses. We're buying below that.
