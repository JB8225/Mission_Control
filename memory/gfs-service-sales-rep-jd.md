## About GFS Fire Pros

GFS Fire Pros is a 40-year-old commercial fire protection company in Dallas-Fort Worth. We inspect, install, and service fire alarm systems, sprinkler systems, and suppression equipment for commercial properties across the DFW metroplex. Our clients include national general contractors like Balfour Beatty, Rogers-O'Brien, and JE Dunn.

Under new ownership (2026), we're launching **GFS SafeGuard™** — a monthly subscription program that converts one-time inspection customers into recurring service contracts. This role is the tip of the spear.

---

## The Role

**Title:** Service Sales Representative
**Type:** Full-time | Base + Commission
**Location:** Dallas-Fort Worth, TX (field-based)
**Reports to:** Mark Bryan, President of Sales

You will convert GFS's existing customer base — hundreds of commercial buildings that have had GFS install or inspect their systems — into SafeGuard monthly subscribers. This is warm outreach, not cold calls. Every person you contact already knows GFS and has worked with us before.

This is not a traditional sales role. You're not pitching a new product to strangers. You're helping existing customers understand why a service contract is better than what they're doing now (which is usually nothing until something breaks or they fail an inspection).

---

## What You'll Do

**Day-to-Day:**
- Work through GFS's completed job database (existing customers, sorted by building type, last service date, and system type)
- Contact building owners, property managers, and facilities directors by phone, email, and in-person visits
- Explain the SafeGuard program tiers (Bronze / Silver / Gold) and recommend the right fit for each building
- Deliver proposals (one-pager per building, pre-built by ops)
- Close service contracts and hand off to the operations team for scheduling
- Track your pipeline and conversions in our CRM

**Target:**
- Month 1: 10 SafeGuard contracts signed
- Month 3: 30 contracts signed (~$10,500–$21,000/month in new MRR at Bronze tier)
- Month 12: 100+ active contracts under management

**Commission structure:**
- $150–$300 per contract signed (based on tier)
- Residual bonus at 12 months if contract is still active
- Performance bonus at 50 / 100 / 200 contract milestones

## Why This Role Is Unusual

Most sales jobs have you hunting from scratch. This one is different:

1. **The database is already built.** GFS has completed jobs going back decades. Every building that's had GFS work is a warm lead.
2. **The compliance angle sells itself.** NFPA 72 and NFPA 25 require annual inspections. If a building owner doesn't have a service contract, they're already out of compliance or one failed inspection away from a problem. You're not selling — you're solving.
3. **The product is sticky.** Once a building is on SafeGuard, they don't cancel. The inspection is mandatory, the relationship is established, and switching costs are real.
4. **The upside compounds.** Every contract you sign stays on the books. In year 2, you're still earning on the contracts you closed in year 1.
