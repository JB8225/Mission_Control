# DFW Independent Commercial Security — GFS Fire Pros Referral Partners
*Researched: 2026-03-24 | Subagent overnight work*

**Purpose:** Identify 3–5 independent (non-PE-backed) commercial security companies in DFW that would be ideal cross-referral partners for GFS Fire Pros post-acquisition.

**Why this matters:** Fire protection and commercial security are natural complements. A building that needs fire suppression/alarm work often also needs access control, video surveillance, and intrusion systems — and vice versa. Cross-referral deals can generate incremental revenue without additional sales cost. Independent shops are preferred over PE-backed rollups (Summit, Pye-Barker, etc.) because they're more likely to do gentleman's agreements and protect referral relationships rather than absorb competitors.
