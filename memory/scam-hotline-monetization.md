### TIER 1 — LAUNCH NOW (0–30 days, minimal work)

#### 1A. Book Sales — Print + eBook
**Revenue model:** One-time purchase. $14.99–$19.99 print / $7.99–$9.99 eBook
**Effort:** Low — book is written. Needs: Amazon KDP setup, cover design if not done, ISBN linked to eBook
**Revenue estimate:** 100 sales/month @ $12 avg = $1,200/month passive
**Why it matters:** Book sales are the bottom of the funnel — they build a buyer list. Every buyer is a warm lead for everything above.
**Action:** Confirm ISBN is active on Amazon KDP. If not, set up in 1 session.

#### 1B. Affiliate Revenue (passive, embedded)
**Revenue model:** Commission per referral. $20–$100+ per signup depending on product.
**Effort:** Low — add links to website, book landing page, email footer
**Top affiliate programs:**

| Product | Commission | Fit |
|---|---|---|
| **Aura** (identity protection) | $10–$25/signup | Perfect — scam victims need this |
| **LifeLock** | $10–$20/signup | Same customer, brand recognition |
| **1Password** | Recurring commission | Chapter 10 ("Build Digital Armor") natural placement |
| **DeleteMe** (data broker removal) | ~$20/signup | Direct fit — scam prevention tool |
| **Experian IdentityWorks** | $15–$30/signup | Credit monitoring angle |

**Revenue estimate:** 500 website visitors/month × 2% conversion × $20 avg = $200–$500/month passive
**Action:** Sign up for Aura, 1Password, and DeleteMe affiliate programs. Embed in website resource page + book landing page.

#### 1C. Sentinel Network Premium Tier
**Revenue model:** Monthly subscription. $9.99–$19.99/month
**Effort:** Low — free group already exists. Paywalled tier via Facebook Groups paid subscription or Patreon/Memberful
**What the premium tier gets:**
- Monthly live Q&A with JB (1 hour, Zoom)
- "Scam Alert" weekly email — new scams, red flags, what to watch for
- Access to Sally (AI scam-check tool) for members
- Private inner circle group (smaller, more intimate)
- Early access to new content and tools

**Revenue estimate:** 200 members @ $9.99/month = $1,998/month recurring
**Why this works:** The free group builds trust. The paid tier captures the most engaged 5–10% who want more access.
**Action:** Survey the free group — "Would you pay $9.99/month for [list above]?" If 20+ yes's, launch.

#### 2B. Corporate Training — Banks, Credit Unions, HR
**Revenue model:** Per-workshop or per-seat licensing. $2,500–$10,000/engagement
**Effort:** Medium — adapt the book into a 60–90 minute presentation deck
**Who buys:**
- **Banks and credit unions** — required to educate customers on fraud. Many have compliance training budgets.
- **HR departments** — employee fraud prevention is a liability issue. C-suite targeted by BEC (Business Email Compromise).
- **Senior living facilities** — elder fraud is epidemic. Facilities are legally motivated to protect residents.
- **Insurance companies** — fraud education reduces claims.

**Why JB wins here:** He's a published author, founder of a named hotline, and has a personal story. That's a speaker with credentials, not a generic trainer.

**Revenue estimate:** 2 engagements/month @ $3,500 avg = $7,000/month
**First move:** Contact 3 local DFW credit unions + 2 large employers. Offer a free 30-minute lunch-and-learn. Convert the room into paid training buyers.

#### 2C. Speaking — Conferences, Associations, Podcasts
**Revenue model:** Paid keynotes $2,500–$15,000. Podcast appearances are free but build authority + book sales.
**Effort:** Low setup (speaker one-pager + topic list), high time commitment per engagement
**Best targets:**
- AARP events (elder fraud audience)
- State bar associations (attorney CLE on client fraud)
- Financial planning conferences (CFP, NAPFA)
- HR/benefits conferences (SHRM)
- Credit union industry events (CUNA, state leagues)
- Fraud/risk management conferences

**Revenue estimate (when active):** 1–2 keynotes/month @ $5,000 avg = $5,000–$10,000/month
**Note:** This is a 6–12 month build. Podcast appearances first → build demand → command fees.
**First move:** Book 5 podcast appearances (scam prevention, personal finance, entrepreneurship podcasts). Use those recordings to build a speaker reel.

**Effort:** High — needs engineering (JB + Claude Code)

**Revenue estimate (B2B):** 5 credit union licenses @ $500/month = $2,500/month recurring
**Note:** This is a significant build. Park until GFS + Resolved are producing. Flag for 2027 roadmap.

#### 3B. Government / Nonprofit Grants
**Revenue model:** Grant funding for fraud education programs
**Examples:** FTC consumer education grants, AARP Foundation grants, state attorney general programs, FINRA Foundation
**Effort:** High — grant writing is a full-time job. Needs a grant writer.
**Revenue estimate:** $25K–$100K per grant. Unpredictable timing.
**Recommendation:** Pursue only if a grant writer volunteers or if a nonprofit structure makes sense. Not a primary revenue path.

#### 3C. The Sentinel Network — Premium Membership Tiers
**Revenue model:** Tiered subscription
**Build this out once the free group hits 1,000+ members:**
- **Bronze ($9.99/month):** Weekly scam alert + Sally access
- **Silver ($24.99/month):** Everything + monthly live Q&A with JB
- **Gold ($49.99/month):** Everything + 1:1 session with JB (30 min, quarterly)
**Revenue estimate (at 500 paid members, blended $15/month):** $7,500/month
