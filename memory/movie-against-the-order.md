# Against the Order — Movie Project
*Last updated: 2026-03-23*

## Project Overview
**Title:** Against the Order
**Type:** Character-driven sports drama
**Subject:** Benji Gil — former MLB first-round pick, winter ball legend, championship coach
**Stage:** Pre-script development. Preparing to align with Benji, then attach development producer.

## Core Theme
*Purpose isn't granted by permission — it's proven through endurance.*

## Story Spine
A former MLB first-round pick struggles to find lasting security in the U.S. system. What begins as honoring his Mexican roots by playing winter ball becomes survival, then reveals itself as his true calling — leadership. After success, betrayal, near-breakthroughs, and continued rejection, he finally finds redemption coaching his son to a championship — not for validation, but dignity. The story ends not in a stadium, but with quiet peace on a beach, as kids play baseball in the distance.

## Emotional Core
- Identity → Survival → Calling
- Success without security
- Rejection without bitterness
- Redemption through fatherhood, not fame
- Ending in peace, not spotlight

## Key Tone Notes
- Grounded, not glossy
- Emotional restraint, no speeches
- Faith shown through behavior, not preaching
- Mexican baseball portrayed with dignity and seriousness
- Victory = peace, not revenge

## Showcase Scenes (Written)
1. Childhood lemon tree scene with his mother — lessons about faith and compassion
2. Championship father/coach confrontation scene
3. Celebration → lights shut off → locker room → hotel ballroom
4. Final epilogue on the beach

## Real Life Details (Benji Gil)
- Former MLB first-round pick — struggled to find lasting security in U.S. system
- Played winter ball in Mexico (Culiacán) — started as honoring roots, became survival, became calling
- MLB coaching opportunity: Brought in by Joe Maddon with the Angels — Maddon fired mid-season — no growth path (new managers bring their own coaches)

- 2026: Won the championship AGAIN with the Charros de Jalisco — beat the Tomateros in a 4-game sweep
- Championship win adds: continued legacy, renewed relevance, more complexity to "has he found peace or is he still chasing?"
