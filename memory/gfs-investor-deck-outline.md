**Title:** How We Build from $19M to $25M+

**Three phases:**

**Phase 1: Year 1 — Lock the installed base**
- Convert existing install customers to inspection contracts
- SafeGuard program: tiered Bronze/Silver/Gold service agreements
- Dedicated Service Sales Rep hired Day 1
- Target: 100+ new inspection contracts in Year 1

**Phase 2: Years 2–3 — Build recurring revenue division**
- Standalone ITM (inspection/testing/maintenance) P&L
- Monitoring partnerships — white-label recurring MRR
- Property manager outreach — shift from GC clients to owner-operators
- Inspection software: InspectPoint or ServiceTrade

**Phase 3: Years 3–5 — Platform + exit**
- Tuck-in acquisitions of small DFW fire inspection shops
- Territory expansion: Fort Worth, Plano, Arlington, Irving
- Position for PE strategic sale at 6–10x EBITDA

**The multiple math:**
- Construction-only EBITDA: valued at 3–5x
- 25%+ recurring (ITM): valued at 6.5–8x
- **Same earnings. Better mix. $10–18M more at exit.**
