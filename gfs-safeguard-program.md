## PART 3: HOW TO BUILD IT — STEP BY STEP

### Step 1: Pick Your Monitoring Partner (Month 1-2)
You don't build a monitoring center — you partner with one.
GFS white-labels their service under the GFS SafeGuard brand.
Cost to GFS: $8–$15/account/month
You charge customer: $50–$75/month for monitoring component
**Net margin on monitoring: ~$40–$60/account/month**

**Who to call:**
- **Alarm.com** (commercial partner program) — alarmdotcom.com/pro
- **Monitoring America** — monitoringamerica.com/pricing — wholesale from $3.50/account
- **COPS Monitoring** — copsmonitoring.com — UL-listed, dealer program
- **Affiliated Monitoring** — affiliated.com — strong commercial focus
- **Bold Group / Manitou** — boldgroup.com — enterprise-grade, full white-label

First call: Ask for their commercial dealer/wholesale program. Tell them you're launching a subscription service for a $19M fire protection company in DFW with 200+ commercial accounts. You'll get their attention.

---

### Step 2: Pick Your Inspection Software (Month 1)
This runs the entire operation. Pick one and go.

**Top 3 options:**

**InspectPoint** (inspectpoint.com)
- Purpose-built for fire protection
- AI assistant that drafts reports, saves 15-20 min/inspection
- Customer portal included
- Used by 15,000+ fire protection pros, 4.5M inspections processed
- Cost: ~$200–$400/month based on volume
- **Best for GFS — purpose-built, AI-native**

**ServiceTrade** (servicetrade.com)
- Broader field service platform (fire is one use case)
- Strong quoting, scheduling, customer communication
- Better if you want one platform for construction AND service
- Cost: ~$300–$600/month
- **Best if you want construction + service in one system**

**FieldEdge** (fieldedge.com)
- Strong QuickBooks integration
- Good for smaller service operations
- Cost: ~$100–$300/month

**Recommendation:** Start with InspectPoint for the service division. It's purpose-built, AI-native, and the industry standard for fire protection ITM.

### Step 3: Price Your Contracts (Month 2)
**The formula:**

1. Calculate your cost per building per year:
   - Tech labor: (hours per inspection × loaded hourly rate)
   - Vehicle: ~$0.70/mile
   - Monitoring wholesale cost: $8–15/month
   - Software overhead per account: ~$2/month
   - Overhead allocation: ~20% of direct cost

2. Target gross margin: **65–75%**

3. Work backward to monthly price
