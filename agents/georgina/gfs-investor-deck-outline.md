## ⚠️ FLAGS FOR JB / DANNY — REVIEW BEFORE APPROVING

**FLAG 1 — IRR Numbers (Slides 7 & 9)**
The IRR figures (~99%, ~103%, ~107%) are extraordinarily high and will invite skepticism from sophisticated investors. Even with SBA leverage, triple-digit IRRs are unusual. **Recommend: have Danny verify the IRR calculation methodology and confirm these are correct.** If they're right, they're right — but they need to be bulletproof when questioned.

**FLAG 2 — Estimated Remaining Debt at Year 5 ($5,500,000)**
This figure is used in every exit calculation. It is listed as an *estimate*. The SBA 7(a) and SBA 504 amortization schedules during standby vs. full-service periods could move this number significantly. **Recommend: get exact amortization tables from Danny and lock this number.**

**FLAG 3 — "Year 2 ROI Recovery" Claim**
The document states "return of investment achieved partway through Year 2 from distributions alone." Math checks out ($29,182 Year 1 + ~$20,818 into Year 2 = $50K recovered by ~Q3–Q4 Year 2). **Confirm this is a selling point JB wants to emphasize** — if so, it's powerful and should get its own callout.

**FLAG 4 — No Scenario A/B Label in Raw Content**
The raw doc references "Scenario A — Maximum SBA Leverage (PRIMARY SCENARIO)" but there is no Scenario B shown. Either there was a second scenario that was cut, or this label is vestigial. **Recommend removing "Scenario A" language unless Scenario B exists and should be added.**
