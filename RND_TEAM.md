## HOW IT WORKS

**Nightly (2am CST) — when there's a meaty topic:**
1. Each analyst reviews DASHBOARD.md + recent daily logs
2. Each generates 3 ideas/recommendations from their lens
3. They "debate" — each challenges the others' top idea
4. Ace synthesizes into a Final Memo
5. Memo delivered to JB in morning brief

**Output: The R&D Memo**
Filed in `memory/rnd-memos/YYYY-MM-DD.md`
Structured as:
- Top 3 opportunities identified
- The debate (where analysts disagreed)
- Final recommendation with confidence level
- One action item for JB

- Every idea must connect to revenue, retention, or competitive advantage
- No idea survives without being challenged by at least one other analyst
- Memos are recommendations only — JB decides what to act on
- Ace flags any idea that conflicts with current priorities (anti-distraction protocol)
